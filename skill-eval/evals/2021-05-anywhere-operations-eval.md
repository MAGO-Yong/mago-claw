## What Was Done Well

1. **Strong quantitative grounding**: Zoom 326% growth, Teams 20M→145M users—these numbers make the trend's scale vivid
2. **"What actually happened" honesty**: Gartner predicted 40% AO adoption by 2023; the report honestly assesses the more nuanced reality (hybrid dominance)
3. **Telemedicine example is excellent**: 30% of primary care visits telehealth by 2021 is a striking, concrete data point
4. **R2 on digital experience monitoring is differentiated**: DEM is underappreciated and the report correctly identifies it as the hardest/most valuable component

## What Was Done Poorly

1. **Security risks underexplored**: The massive attack surface expansion from remote work is mentioned briefly but deserves more depth
2. **Small business context missing**: Most examples are large enterprise; AO challenges for SMBs are different
3. **Vendor list in R2 is thin**: Only three DEM vendors mentioned; market is larger

## Improvement Suggestions

1. Add a cybersecurity cost analysis—what did the anywhere operations transition cost in breach incidents?
2. Include an SMB perspective on AO challenges
3. Expand the DEM vendor landscape and include a comparison of approaches
