## What Was Done Poorly

1. **Open-source models underemphasized**: Llama 2/3, Mistral, Falcon—open-source GenAI is increasingly important for enterprises concerned about vendor lock-in and data privacy
2. **Cost analysis missing**: GenAI API costs are significant; no analysis of cost curves or break-even scenarios
3. **R2 is slightly thin on specific failure cases**: Could include one case where GenAI deployment caused real harm (misinformation, bias)

## Improvement Suggestions

1. Add open-source GenAI model landscape (Llama, Mistral) as an alternative to API-dependent architectures
2. Include GenAI cost analysis—token costs, fine-tuning costs, vs. business value
3. Add a "Cautionary Case" section with one specific documented failure
