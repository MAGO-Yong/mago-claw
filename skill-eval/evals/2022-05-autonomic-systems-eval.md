3. **Mars Rover example**: The light-speed delay reason for autonomic navigation is elegant and memorable

## What Was Done Poorly

1. **This trend has not aged particularly well**: Autonomic systems as a distinct concept was largely absorbed into "AI-driven systems" and "agentic AI"—report could comment on this
2. **AIOps market estimates lack source specificity**: "$11.3 billion to $40.9 billion"—without named sources these are approximations
3. **Legal accountability section is brief**: Given it's flagged as a major risk, the accountability gap deserves more development

## Improvement Suggestions

1. Add a "How This Trend Evolved" section noting how autonomic systems transitioned into broader AI/agentic AI discussions
2. Search for more current (2024-2025) enterprise AIOps outcome data
3. Expand the legal accountability section with specific regulatory developments
