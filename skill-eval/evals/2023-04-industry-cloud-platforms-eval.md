2. **Industry vertical organization**: Healthcare, financial services, retail, manufacturing—immediately relatable structure
3. **FHIR mandate as ICP driver**: Regulatory catalyst explains why the timing was right in 2023
4. **Providence case**: Specific health system with named metrics (51 hospitals, 60% data integration reduction)
5. **"Platform vs. application" boundary**: Nuanced conclusion helps readers understand ICP positioning

## What Was Done Poorly

2. **Gartner prediction follow-up**: The "50% of enterprises by 2027" prediction isn't assessed against 2024 evidence
3. **Financial services ICP examples are thin**: Temenos and Finastra mentioned briefly but not explained well

1. Add a financial services ICP case study (Temenos is the market leader—find a specific bank case)
2. Search for 2024-2025 ICP adoption data to assess the 2027 prediction trajectory
3. Add comparison between ICP and best-of-breed vertical SaaS approaches
