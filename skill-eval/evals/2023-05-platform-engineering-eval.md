## What Was Done Well

1. **DORA metrics**: 2x deployment frequency, 3x faster lead time—authoritative, widely cited; excellent evidence base
2. **4-phase Backstage implementation**: The most specific, actionable implementation guide in any report—catalog → templates → integrations → self-service
3. **"Platform as product" principle**: Treating developers as customers is the key insight; well-stated
4. **Anti-patterns section**: "Platform dictators" is a memorable, useful failure mode warning
5. **American Airlines, Box, Zalando outcomes**: Named, specific, with metrics—highly credible

## What Was Done Poorly

1. **Cognitive load problem could be quantified better**: "40-50% on infrastructure" needs source; this is a frequently cited estimate of unclear origin
2. **Platform team sizing guidance**: "5-20% of engineering headcount" without sourcing; guidance is helpful but uncalibrated
3. **Cost of platform engineering investment**: No estimate of what building this costs—readers need to make the business case

## Improvement Suggestions

1. Source the "40-50% time on infrastructure" statistic with original research
2. Add a business case template: "Platform engineering investment vs. developer productivity gains"
3. Mention the CNCF Platforms Working Group—they've published valuable guidelines
