## What Was Done Well

1. **Technology table**: Six wireless technologies with key characteristics and use cases—perfect reference tool
2. **BMW Leipzig case**: Specific, named, with quantified outcome (30% productivity improvement)—excellent
3. **R2 decision framework**: Choose Wi-Fi 6 when... / Choose Private 5G when... / Hybrid approach—immediately actionable
4. **"Realization" framing**: Correctly emphasizes business outcome over connectivity infrastructure
5. **Cost reality section**: 5-10x higher TCO for private 5G vs. Wi-Fi—readers need this to make decisions

## What Was Done Poorly

1. **Starlink aviation coverage needs updating**: "600+ aircraft by 2023"—this has grown significantly since
2. **LPWAN section is thin**: LPWAN is the most widely deployed IoT wireless technology globally; deserves more depth
3. **CBRS spectrum explanation**: CBRS (Citizens Broadband Radio Service) is key to US private 5G but unexplained for non-expert readers

## Improvement Suggestions

1. Add a "Getting Started with Private 5G" guide—most enterprises don't know where to begin
2. Expand LPWAN section with more use case examples
3. Search for 2024/2025 private 5G deployment data to update adoption trajectory
