## What Was Done Well

1. **WeChat reference model**: Comprehensive, accurate, and provides essential Asian market context
2. **Enterprise superapp insight**: Many readers won't have thought of Teams/Salesforce as "superapps"—this reframing adds real value
3. **"Why Western Superapps Are Harder"**: Four-reason analysis is concise and correct—regulatory, competition, consumer expectations, cultural
4. **Enterprise decision questions**: The four questions (command center, mini-apps, custom builds, data governance) are immediately applicable
5. **X (Twitter) update**: Including Elon Musk's "everything app" ambition is current and relevant

## What Was Done Poorly

1. **"50% daily active users of superapps" prediction assessment is incomplete**: The report raises the question but doesn't assess whether the prediction is on track
2. **Mini-program technical architecture section could be simpler**: WXML/WXSS is unnecessary detail for most readers
3. **PayPal "super app" failure analysis**: PayPal's superapp attempt was a significant strategic failure worth more analysis

## Improvement Suggestions

1. Add a "What Happened to PayPal's Super App" post-mortem—it's a valuable failure case
2. Assess the Gartner 2027 prediction against current adoption data
3. Remove technical detail (WXML/WXSS) in favor of business-level mini-program explanation
