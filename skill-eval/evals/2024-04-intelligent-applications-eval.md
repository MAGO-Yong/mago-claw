## What Was Done Poorly

1. **Apple Intelligence briefly mentioned**: It's a major intelligent application platform that deserves more attention
2. **Privacy implications of intelligent applications**: Personalization requires data; privacy trade-off is barely mentioned
3. **B2B vs. B2C intelligent application dynamics**: The report mixes these without noting the different challenges

## Improvement Suggestions

1. Add Apple Intelligence as a platform case (it affects 1B+ device users)
2. Include privacy considerations for intelligent applications—personalization data governance
3. Create a "B2B Enterprise" vs. "Consumer" section to help readers understand which examples apply to them
