## What Was Done Well

1. **"Input→Output vs. Goal→Plan→Execute" comparison**: The clearest explanation of the fundamental agentic AI shift
2. **Evolution timeline**: ChatGPT (2022) → Computer Use (2024) → Operator (2025)—shows momentum without hype
3. **Salesforce Agentforce launch**: Specific, with business impact (stock up 20% on announcement) showing real market belief
4. **Prompt injection for agents**: Correctly identified as more dangerous for agents than chatbots—original and important
5. **Three autonomy levels**: Supervised → exception-based → full autonomy is the right deployment progression framework

## What Was Done Poorly

1. **Market size reliability**: "$14.9 billion by 2025" from SuperAGI (not a primary analyst) should be flagged
2. **Devin controversy**: The report says "controversial" without explaining why—Devin's claims were disputed
3. **Reversibility discussion is thin**: This is one of the hardest problems in agent governance—deserves more depth

## Improvement Suggestions

1. Explain why Devin was controversial (independent tests found significant limitations vs. claimed capability)
2. Deep dive on reversibility—which agent actions can/cannot be reversed and what to do in each case
3. Source the market size with a primary analyst report (Gartner, IDC, Forrester)
